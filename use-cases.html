Everyday Users

Send crypto to anyone by @name — no more copying 42-character addresses. Your balance, transaction history, and counterparties are hidden from block explorers by default.

  • Send to @name instead of 0x addresses
  • Balance hidden from public block explorers
  • Stealth address per sender-receiver pair
  • Phishing risk eliminated — no address sharing
  • Works on all major chains from one wallet

Institutional Finance

Treasury operations, OTC settlements, and custody flows require confidentiality. AmericanFortress provides compliant, auditable privacy — not anonymous mixing. Full traceability for authorized parties.

  • Selective disclosure for compliance audits

  • Brinks custody integration (exclusive partner)
  • SEC and MiCA regulatory alignment
