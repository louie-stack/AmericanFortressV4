FAQs

What is AmericanFortress?

AmericanFortress is a patented privacy infrastructure that lets people, apps, and institutions send by @name while protecting balances and counterparties.

How does it reduce phishing risk?

Every sender-receiver pair gets a unique stealth route behind a simple @name. This removes risky address copy-paste behavior and reduces wallet-targeting exposure.

What is Send-to-Name?

Instead of copying long addresses, users send to a verified @name. AmericanFortress handles secure address resolution and privacy routing automatically.

Can I integrate this into my app or wallet?

Yes. The SDK is built for quick integration into wallets, exchanges, and applications with developer-friendly implementation across major chains.

Who is AmericanFortress built for?

  • Everyday Users: Send crypto safely just by typing a name.
  • Developers & AI Builders: Embed secure send-to-name in your app or AI with a few lines of code.
  • Institutions: Use our custody-ready, KYC-compliant wallet.
  • Regulators: A privacy-preserving, fraud-reducing framework for compliance.

Is it compliance-ready?

Yes. The system is designed for selective disclosure and architecture-level AML/KYC compatibility, privacy without abandoning institutional requirements.

How do AI agents use this?

AI agents can send and receive value through identity-safe rails without exposing strategy wallets, counterparties, or balances by default.

What is $AF used for?

$AF secures name registry operations, supports protocol-level utility, and aligns long-term network incentives including privacy infrastructure growth.
